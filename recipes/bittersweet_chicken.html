Bittersweet Chicken

Description

Bittersweet chicken features crispy, bite-sized chicken tossed in a bold sauce. The sauce balances sugary sweetness with a tangy, slightly bitter bite.

It tastes rich and savory, creating a delicious flavor explosion in every bite.

Ingredients

For the chicken:

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

For the bittersweet sauce:

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp ketchup (for color and tang)
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar or apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Prep the chicken: Cut the chicken breast into small cubes. Toss the chicken pieces in a bag with the flour, salt, and pepper until fully coated.
  2. Make the sauce: In a small bowl, mix the soy sauce, honey, ketchup, water, vinegar, and cornstarch. Stir until no dry lumps of cornstarch remain.
  3. Cook the chicken: Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces. Cook for 6-8 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Add the sauce: Pour the sauce over the cooked chicken in the pan. Lower the heat to medium.
  5. Thicken: Stir continuously for 2-3 minutes. The cornstarch will make the sauce bubble and turn thick and glossy. Remove from heat when the sauce coats the chicken.
