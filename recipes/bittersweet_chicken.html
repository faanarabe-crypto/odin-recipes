Bittersweet Chicken

Description

Bittersweet chicken features crispy, bite-sized chicken tossed in a bold sauce. The sauce balances sugary sweetness with a tangy, slightly bitter bite.

It tastes rich and savory, creating a delicious flavor explosion in every bite.

Ingredients

For the chicken:

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

For the bittersweet sauce:

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp ketchup (for color and tang)
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar or apple cider vinegar
